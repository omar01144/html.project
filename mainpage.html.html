Artificial intelligence

Artificial intelligence is the behavior and specific characteristics of computer programs that mimic human mental capabilities and working patterns. Among the most important of these characteristics is the ability to learn, infer, and react to situations not programmed in the machine. However, this term is controversial due to the lack of a specific definition of intelligence.
